Galway Cathedral

Enjoy the beautiful arches and dome of the Galway Cathedral.

Inside the cathedral is even more beautiful with its elegant stained glass windows.

There is a lovely riverside path from the Galway Cathedral to the Spanish Arch.

Spanish Arch

Check out the Spanish Arch!

Ships passed through the Spanish Arch carrying goods from Spain to unload in the city.

Water no longer flows through the arch, but the arch sits next to a fast flowing river.

City Centre

Walk down the streets of Galway and discover the shops and pubs it has to offer.

Galway is a small city and easily explored by foot. You can get from the Spanish Arch to Galway Cathedral and anywhere else in a matter of minutes!

Galway is a great place to get a Claddagh ring. The traditional Irish ring represents love, loyalty and friendship through the heart, crown and hands, respectively.

The Aran Islands are only a short ferry ride from Galway and it is a trip that I highly recommend!

The Cliffs of Moher

Take a trip to see one of Ireland's top destinations: the Cliffs of Moher.

Located in the Burren region in County Clare, the Cliffs of Moher are just a short trip from Galway.

The highest point of the cliffs is 214m and on a clear day you can see the Aran Islands and Galway, among other places.

The Cliffs of Moher have also been featured in the sixth Harry Potter film.
