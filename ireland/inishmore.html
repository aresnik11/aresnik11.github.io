Rent a Bike

Although Inishmore is the largest of the three Aran Islands, you can easily explore the entire island via bike.

Bike rentals are easy and cheap at Burke's Bicycle Hire. Burke can give you directions on how to get anywhere on the island.

Biking across Inishmore is a great way to really see all that the island has to offer.

Make sure you stop to take in the views along the ride!

Dun Aonghasa

Bike across the island to Dun Aonghasa, an old fort that sits on the edge of a cliff that is 60m high.

The fort does not have any rails or barriers so you can go right up to the edge of the cliff. It can be pretty scary on a windy day so be careful!

The lack of rails allows you to sit on the edge of the cliff and dangle your feet off the edge.

Dun Aonghasa has spectacular views and is a highlight of the island.

The fort also has an interesting history that can be learned about at the visitor center.

Aran Sweater Market

Come shop in the Aran Sweater Market and get your own authentic Aran sweater from one of the Aran Islands!

The store is huge and has a large variety of patterns, colors and price options.
