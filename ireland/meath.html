Newgrange

Explore a prehistoric tomb that is older than Stonehenge and the Egyptian pyramids!

Newgrange is a passage tomb consisting of a large circular mound with stone passages and chambers.

The mound is surrounded by stones at the base including an intricate entrance stone.

On the winter solstice, sunlight enters the chamber through the roof box.

Loughcrew

Climb to the top of a hill to see a megalithic tomb, art and a great view across the Irish midlands!

Loughcrew is a megalithic cemetery that spans several hill-tops.

The tombs were put in the landscape with an eye on astronomy. There is an alignment with the sun on the shortest or longest day of the year.
